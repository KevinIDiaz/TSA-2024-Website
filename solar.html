Solar

Introduction

As the world increasingly shifts towards sustainable energy sources, solar power stands out as one of the leading solutions for homeowners seeking to reduce their carbon footprint and lower energy costs.



Solar energy systems, primarily composed of photovoltaic (PV) panels, absorb sunlight with PV cells converting solar energy into electricity. These cells are interconnected to form modules, with an average solar panel typically comprising around 30 modules. There are two main types of solar panels: industrial and residential. Industrial panels are larger and more efficient, while residential panels are easier to install despite generating slightly less power. The standard colors for solar panels are black and blue, which are more commonly used in industrial and residential settings.
