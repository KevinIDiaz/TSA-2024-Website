What is Green Energy?

Green energy is a form of renewable energy. Green energy is the resources and technologies that benefit the environment. Electricity produced from solar, wind, and geothermal sources are a few examples of where green energy comes from. Green energy products are often bought because they reduce carbon footprints and have zero emissions. Green energy is a popular solution to combating climate change. Coal, oil, and gas are the fossil fuels that contribute the most to global climate change. They make up more than 50 percent of greenhouse gas emissions. Green energy products, which use renewable energy sources that can be found everywhere, produce little to no pollution or greenhouse gasses.
